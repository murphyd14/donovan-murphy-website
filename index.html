About Me

As I near graduation, I am eager to apply my technical skills and collaborative mindset to impactful projects, especially in fields where technology intersects with finance and innovation. I am actively seeking full-time opportunities that allow me to grow as a software engineer while contributing to dynamic teams in the financial or defense industries.
